What We Do

Manufacturing Capabilities

From small molecules to advanced biologics, our manufacturing network covers the full spectrum of pharmaceutical production with precision at every step.

Small Molecule Synthesis

State-of-the-art chemical synthesis facilities producing active pharmaceutical ingredients and finished dosage forms with unmatched consistency and scale.

API synthesis capacity: 500+ metric tons/year
Tablet, capsule, and injectable formulations
Continuous flow chemistry integration
Controlled substance handling (DEA licensed)

Biologics Production

Advanced biomanufacturing platforms for monoclonal antibodies, recombinant proteins, and biosimilars using cutting-edge cell culture technologies.

Mammalian cell culture: 120,000L total capacity
Single-use and stainless steel bioreactors
Protein A chromatography purification
Fill-finish with isolator technology

Cell & Gene Therapy

Purpose-built facilities for autologous and allogeneic cell therapies, viral vector manufacturing, and gene editing applications at clinical and commercial scale.

Viral vector production (AAV, lentiviral)
GMP-grade cleanroom suites (ISO 5/7)
Closed-system autologous processing
Cryopreservation and chain of identity

Advanced Drug Delivery

Innovative formulation and delivery technologies including nanoparticles, liposomes, sustained-release systems, and prefilled syringe/auto-injector assembly.

Lipid nanoparticle (LNP) encapsulation
Microsphere & implant manufacturing
Device assembly: auto-injectors & pens
Lyophilization (freeze-drying) capacity

Uncompromising Standards

Quality & Compliance

Every medicine we manufacture meets the highest global regulatory standards. Quality is not just a requirement -- it is the foundation of patient trust.

cGMP Excellence

All facilities operate under current Good Manufacturing Practice regulations with continuous improvement programs and real-time monitoring systems.

FDA / EMA Approved

Successfully passed 50+ regulatory inspections. Pre-approved by FDA, EMA, PMDA, TGA, and Health Canada with zero critical findings in the past 5 years.

ISO Certified

ISO 9001:2015 quality management, ISO 14001:2015 environmental management, and ISO 45001:2018 occupational health and safety across all sites.

Environmental Standards

EHS management systems exceed regulatory requirements. LEED-certified new builds and ongoing environmental monitoring at every manufacturing site.

End-to-End Reliability

Supply Chain Excellence

Our resilient, technology-driven supply chain ensures every dose reaches patients on time -- from API sourcing through final mile delivery across more than 150 countries.

Cold Chain Logistics

Temperature-controlled supply chain from -80C to +25C with validated packaging, GPS-enabled monitoring, and automated deviation alerts.

Real-Time Tracking

End-to-end shipment visibility through IoT sensors, blockchain-based serialization, and a centralized control tower for proactive risk management.

Industry 4.0

Innovation in Manufacturing

Harnessing the latest technologies to drive efficiency, quality, and speed -- transforming how medicines are made for the next generation of healthcare.

AI-Powered Quality Control

Machine learning algorithms analyze millions of data points in real-time to detect anomalies before they impact product quality. Predictive models reduce batch failures by 40%.

Continuous Manufacturing

Replacing batch-based processes with end-to-end continuous manufacturing lines. 60% reduction in production time and 30% smaller physical footprint per dose.

Digital Twins

Virtual replicas of every manufacturing line enable real-time simulation, process optimization, and predictive maintenance -- minimizing downtime and maximizing throughput.

Robotic Automation

Collaborative robots handle repetitive tasks from vial inspection to packaging, improving precision, reducing contamination risk, and enabling 24/7 operations.

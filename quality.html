Integrated Systems

Quality Management
System.

Our comprehensive Quality Management System ensures every product meets the highest standards of safety, efficacy, and purity throughout its lifecycle.

Quality by Design

We embed quality into every stage of product development using QbD principles. From defining critical quality attributes to establishing design spaces, our proactive approach ensures robust manufacturing processes that consistently deliver safe, effective medicines.

CQA Definition Design Space Control Strategy

Risk-Based Approach

Our ICH Q9-aligned risk management framework identifies, assesses, and mitigates quality risks across the product lifecycle. Using tools like FMEA, HACCP, and fault tree analysis, we proactively address potential issues before they impact patients.

FMEA HACCP Risk Mitigation

Continuous Improvement

We are committed to ongoing enhancement of our quality systems through ICH Q10 pharmaceutical quality system principles. CAPA processes, management reviews, and knowledge management drive year-over-year improvements in product quality and process performance.

Drug Safety

Pharmacovigilance.

Our global pharmacovigilance organization monitors the safety of our medicines throughout their lifecycle, using advanced analytics to detect, assess, and manage potential risks to patients.

1

Drug Safety Monitoring

Continuous surveillance of all marketed products using real-world data, electronic health records, and spontaneous reporting systems to identify potential safety signals.

  • Real-world evidence analysis
  • Spontaneous reporting
  • Literature monitoring
2

Adverse Event Reporting

Rigorous case processing and regulatory reporting within mandated timelines. Our global intake system ensures no report goes unassessed, with 24/7 availability for serious events.

  • 24/7 global intake
  • 15-day serious case reporting
  • Automated ICSRs
3

Signal Detection

AI-powered signal detection combines disproportionality analysis, data mining, and machine learning to identify emerging safety trends faster than traditional methods alone.

  • AI-powered analytics
  • Disproportionality analysis
  • Cumulative review
4

Risk Management

Comprehensive risk management plans (RMPs) and risk evaluation and mitigation strategies (REMS) ensure identified risks are minimized and benefits are maximized for every product.

  • Risk management plans
  • REMS programs
  • Periodic safety updates

Featured Programs

Key pipeline highlights.

Cardiovascular Phase III

Veloranib (MKN-1042)

Chronic Heart Failure with Reduced Ejection Fraction

Mechanism of Action

Selective cardiac myosin activator that enhances the contractile force of the heart without increasing calcium levels or myocardial oxygen demand.

Pivotal Trial

CARDIAC-HOPE: Randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled Phase III trial in 4,200 patients across 350 sites globally.

Expected Milestones

Topline data readout Q3 2026. Regulatory submission planned H1 2027.

Enrollment complete

Immunology Phase III

Talirumab (MKN-2187)

Moderate-to-Severe Rheumatoid Arthritis

Mechanism of Action

Dual IL-17A/F inhibitor with best-in-class selectivity, neutralizing both isoforms to achieve superior inflammation control.

Pivotal Trial

RESOLVE-RA: Phase III head-to-head study vs. standard of care in 2,800 biologic-naive and biologic-experienced patients.

Expected Milestones

Primary endpoint analysis Q4 2026. Breakthrough Therapy designation under FDA review.

Breakthrough Therapy candidate

Oncology Phase II/III

Onitresib (MKN-3301)

KRAS G12C+ Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer

Mechanism of Action

Highly selective covalent KRAS G12C inhibitor with demonstrated CNS penetration, addressing a key limitation of existing therapies.

Pivotal Trial

TITAN-Lung: Adaptive Phase II/III registrational trial in 1,600 patients with previously treated KRAS G12C-mutant NSCLC.

Expected Milestones

Interim efficacy analysis Q2 2026. Potential accelerated approval filing Q1 2027 based on ORR data.

Fast Track designation granted

Pipeline News

Recent pipeline updates.

March 28, 2026 Regulatory Milestone

Thrombexar (MKN-1005) Receives FDA Priority Review

The FDA has accepted our New Drug Application for Thrombexar and granted Priority Review designation for the prevention of venous thromboembolism. PDUFA date set for September 2026.

March 15, 2026 Phase Transition

Onitresib (MKN-3301) Advances to Registrational Phase III Cohort

Based on compelling Phase II efficacy data showing a 42% objective response rate, we are expanding the TITAN-Lung trial with a registrational Phase III cohort of 1,200 additional patients.

February 22, 2026 Data Readout

Positive Phase II Results for Cognivant (MKN-4112) in Early Alzheimer's

MKN-4112 met its primary endpoint in a 700-patient Phase II trial, demonstrating a 27% reduction in cognitive decline compared to placebo over 18 months, with a favorable safety profile.

January 30, 2026 Designation

Remisara (MKN-2055) Granted EMA Accelerated Assessment for Psoriatic Arthritis

The European Medicines Agency has granted Accelerated Assessment for our Marketing Authorization Application for Remisara, recognizing the significant therapeutic benefit over existing treatments.

January 10, 2026 Trial Initiation

First Patient Dosed in CARDIAC-HOPE Phase III Trial for Veloranib

The first patient has been dosed in the global CARDIAC-HOPE pivotal trial evaluating Veloranib in chronic heart failure with reduced ejection fraction, marking a major milestone for our cardiovascular portfolio.

Common Questions

Frequently asked questions.

Find quick answers to the most common inquiries we receive.

If you experience or become aware of a side effect related to any Makana Health product, you can report it 24/7 by calling our Pharmacovigilance hotline at 1-800-555-9111 or by emailing safety@makanahealth.com. You may also report adverse events through the FDA's MedWatch program. Your report will be handled confidentially and helps us monitor the ongoing safety of our medicines. Healthcare professionals can also submit reports through our HCP portal.

Product availability varies by country and is subject to local regulatory approvals. Visit our Products page for the current list of approved medications. If a product is not yet available in your region, our Commercial Operations team can provide information on anticipated timelines or compassionate use programs. Contact commercial@makanahealth.com for region-specific availability.

Full prescribing information, including approved indications, dosing, warnings, and safety data, is available on each product's individual page within our Products section. Healthcare professionals can also access detailed product monographs, clinical data summaries, and safety updates through our HCP Portal. For additional medical information, contact our Medical Information team at 1-800-555-1234.

Information about our active clinical trials, including eligibility criteria and participating sites, is available on our Clinical Trials page. You can also search for our trials on ClinicalTrials.gov. To discuss whether a trial may be appropriate for you, please speak with your healthcare provider. For site selection or investigator inquiries, contact clinicaldev@makanahealth.com.

Yes. The Makana Patient Support Program provides financial assistance, co-pay support, and free medication to eligible patients who cannot afford their treatment. Eligibility is based on income, insurance status, and residency. Visit our Patients page for program details and enrollment forms, or call our Patient Support team at 1-800-555-1234 (Mon-Fri, 8am-8pm ET).

Our Medical Information team is available to answer scientific and medical questions about Makana Health products. Healthcare professionals and patients can request information by calling 1-800-555-1234 (Mon-Fri, 8am-6pm ET), emailing medinfo@makanahealth.com, or submitting a request through the HCP Portal. Responses are typically provided within 24-48 hours for standard requests.

For financial information, SEC filings, earnings reports, and shareholder services, visit our Investors page. Analysts and institutional investors can reach our Investor Relations team at investors@makanahealth.com or by calling +1 (808) 555-4800. To receive email alerts for press releases and SEC filings, sign up on our investor portal.

Makana Health actively seeks partnerships across drug discovery, co-development, licensing, and distribution. We are particularly interested in collaborations in oncology, immunology, neuroscience, and rare diseases. To explore partnership opportunities, contact our Business Development team at partnerships@makanahealth.com. Please include a brief summary of the proposed collaboration and relevant data or background information.

All open positions are listed on our Careers page, where you can search by location, function, and experience level. We encourage you to create a profile and submit your application online. For questions about the application process, recruitment events, or internship programs, contact careers@makanahealth.com. Makana Health is an equal opportunity employer committed to building a diverse and inclusive workforce.

We take data privacy seriously and comply with applicable regulations including GDPR, CCPA, and HIPAA where relevant. All personal information submitted through our website is encrypted in transit and at rest. We collect only the data necessary to respond to your inquiry and do not sell personal information to third parties. For detailed information about our data practices, please review our Privacy Policy or contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@makanahealth.com.

Medical Emergency

If you or someone you know is experiencing a medical emergency, call your local emergency number immediately. In the United States, dial 911. For non-emergency adverse event reporting, call our 24/7 safety hotline at 1-800-555-9111. Do not use this website for urgent medical situations.

Innovation Engines

Technology platforms.

Five powerful technology platforms enable us to discover and develop the next generation of transformative therapies, each unlocking unique mechanisms to treat disease.

Chemistry & Small Molecules

Our industry-leading medicinal chemistry platform leverages AI-driven design and high-throughput screening to identify and optimize small molecule candidates with superior pharmacological profiles and oral bioavailability.

AI-Driven Design HTS ADMET Optimization

Biotherapeutics

Engineered antibodies, bispecific T-cell engagers, and next-generation protein therapeutics designed to target disease pathways with exquisite specificity. Our platform has produced 8 approved medicines.

Monoclonal Antibodies Bispecifics ADCs

xRNA Therapeutics

Silencing disease at its source through RNA interference, antisense oligonucleotides, and mRNA technologies to address previously undruggable targets.

Radioligand Therapy

Delivering targeted radiation directly to cancer cells with precision, sparing healthy tissue and opening new frontiers in precision oncology treatment.

Gene & Cell Therapy

Developing potentially curative one-time treatments by correcting genetic defects or engineering cells to fight disease at its root cause.

HFrEF Management Pathway

1LVEF assessment & NYHA classification
2Initiate ACEi/ARB/ARNI + Beta-blocker
3Add SGLT2i + MRA per guidelines
4Consider Cardivex for persistent symptoms
5Device therapy / advanced HF referral

Moderate-to-Severe RA Pathway

With Immunorel positioning after csDMARD failure

1Confirm diagnosis (ACR/EULAR 2010 criteria)
2Initiate methotrexate +/- HCQ
3Assess response at 12-16 weeks
4Inadequate response: Consider Immunorel
5Monitor DAS28-CRP / treat-to-target

NSCLC Biomarker-Driven Selection

With Oncolys + KRAS G12C companion diagnostic

1Tissue biopsy / liquid biopsy (NGS panel)
2Biomarker identification (EGFR, ALK, KRAS, etc.)
3PD-L1 TPS & TMB assessment
4KRAS G12C+: Consider Oncolys (onitresib)
5Response assessment (RECIST 1.1) q8w

1Cognitive screening (MMSE, MoCA)
2Amyloid confirmation (PET or CSF biomarkers)
3ApoE genotyping & baseline MRI
4Initiate Neuravox with ARIA protocol
5Serial MRI surveillance at weeks 7, 12, 24

Journal Club — March 2026

Monthly curated selection with Makana expert commentary. 4 new publications

Cardiovascular NEJM

Cardiac Myosin Activation in Heart Failure with Reduced Ejection Fraction: The VITALITY-HF Randomized Clinical Trial

Teerlink JR, Diaz R, Felker GM, et al. N Engl J Med. 2026;394(3):215-227.

Key Takeaways:

  • Primary endpoint met: 24% reduction in composite of CV death/HF hospitalization
  • Significant improvement in Kansas City Cardiomyopathy Questionnaire (KCCQ) score
  • Favorable safety profile with low discontinuation rates

Makana Expert Commentary:

"These data solidify cardiac myosin activation as a paradigm shift in HFrEF management, complementing existing GDMT." — Dr. R. Patel, VP Medical Affairs

Immunology Lancet

Dual Cytokine Inhibition in Refractory Rheumatoid Arthritis: ILLUMINATE-RA Phase 3 Results

Smolen JS, Aletaha D, Bijlsma JWJ, et al. Lancet. 2026;407(10340):1812-1824.

Key Takeaways:

  • ACR50 at week 24: 62% vs 31% placebo (p<0.001)
  • Radiographic progression halted in 84% of patients
  • Infections comparable to single-agent biologics

Makana Expert Commentary:

"Dual pathway inhibition addresses the unmet need in patients failing first-line biologics, offering superior joint protection." — Dr. L. Chen, Immunology R&D

Oncology J Clin Oncol

KRAS G12C Inhibition Plus Anti-PD-1 in Previously Treated NSCLC: HORIZON-Lung Phase 2

Skoulidis F, Li BT, Dy GK, et al. J Clin Oncol. 2026;44(8):910-921.

Key Takeaways:

  • ORR 47% in 2L+ KRAS G12C-mutated NSCLC
  • Median PFS 8.4 months vs 4.1 months with docetaxel
  • Manageable safety profile; GI toxicity most common

Makana Expert Commentary:

"Combination with immunotherapy unlocks synergy in KRAS-driven tumors and could redefine 2L NSCLC treatment." — Dr. A. Gupta, Oncology Medical Director

Neuroscience JAMA Neurol

Amyloid Clearance and Clinical Outcomes in Early Alzheimer's Disease: CLARITY-AD Extended Follow-Up

van Dyck CH, Swanson CJ, Aisen P, et al. JAMA Neurol. 2026;83(3):312-325.

Key Takeaways:

  • 35% slowing of clinical decline (CDR-SB) at 18 months sustained at 36 months
  • Amyloid clearance correlates with cognitive benefit (r=0.68)
  • ARIA-E incidence decreases significantly after first 6 months

Makana Expert Commentary:

"Long-term data confirm that early and sustained amyloid reduction translates to meaningful clinical benefit. ARIA management protocols continue to improve." — Dr. S. Kim, Neuroscience Lead

Our Approach

Reimagining Heart Health

Cardiovascular disease remains the leading cause of death worldwide. At Makana Health, we pursue a multi-pronged strategy to develop transformative therapies that address unmet needs across the full spectrum of cardiovascular conditions.

Novel Mechanisms

Targeting first-in-class pathways including selective cardiac myosin inhibition, soluble guanylate cyclase stimulation, and next-generation anti-thrombotic approaches to address root causes of cardiovascular disease.

Precision Medicine

Leveraging genomic insights and biomarker-driven patient stratification to deliver the right therapy to the right patient. Our genetic testing programs identify patients most likely to benefit from targeted interventions.

Digital Cardiology

Integrating AI-powered diagnostics, remote patient monitoring, and digital biomarkers into our clinical programs. Our digital health platform enables real-time cardiac rhythm analysis and early detection of adverse events.

Patient Access

Committed to ensuring cardiovascular therapies reach every patient who needs them. Our global access programs, copay assistance, and partnerships with healthcare systems work to eliminate barriers to care.

Approved 2024 HCM

Cardivex®

makanivol tablets

First-in-class selective cardiac myosin inhibitor approved for the treatment of symptomatic obstructive Hypertrophic Cardiomyopathy (HCM) in adults. Cardivex works by reducing the excessive contractility of the heart muscle that characterizes HCM, a genetic condition affecting approximately 1 in 500 people worldwide.

Mechanism of Action

Makanivol selectively inhibits cardiac myosin ATPase, reducing the number of actin-myosin cross-bridges formed during each cardiac cycle. This targeted approach decreases left ventricular contractility without affecting vascular tone, heart rate, or myocardial relaxation, resulting in reduced left ventricular outflow tract (LVOT) obstruction.

Vasolift®

endorelax capsules

Novel soluble guanylate cyclase (sGC) stimulator for the treatment of Pulmonary Arterial Hypertension (PAH). Vasolift enhances the nitric oxide-sGC-cGMP signaling pathway, a critical regulator of pulmonary vascular tone, and has demonstrated significant improvements in exercise capacity and clinical outcomes in WHO Functional Class II-III patients.

Heart Science

Understanding the Heart

Explore Our Cardiac Focus Areas

Hover over the heart chambers to discover the conditions we are targeting and the therapeutic approaches we are developing to address them.

Left Atrium — Atrial Fibrillation

The left atrium is frequently the origin of atrial fibrillation. Our factor XIa inhibitor MKH-6701 aims to prevent AF-related strokes with a dramatically improved bleeding profile compared to current standard-of-care anticoagulants.

Right Atrium — Cardiac Amyloidosis

Amyloid protein deposits accumulate throughout the heart, leading to restrictive cardiomyopathy. Our gene silencing therapy MKH-7102 targets TTR production at the hepatic level to prevent further amyloid deposition.

Left Ventricle — HCM & Heart Failure

The left ventricle is central to both HCM and Heart Failure. Cardivex targets the excessive contractility of the thickened left ventricular wall in HCM, while MKH-4201 addresses the metabolic dysfunction underlying HFpEF.

Right Ventricle — Pulmonary Hypertension

In PAH, elevated pulmonary pressures place excessive strain on the right ventricle. Vasolift reduces pulmonary vascular resistance by stimulating the sGC pathway, offloading the right ventricle and improving outcomes.

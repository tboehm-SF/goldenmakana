Important Safety Information

INDICATIONS AND USAGE: IMMUNEXIS® is indicated for the treatment of moderate-to-severe atopic dermatitis in patients aged 6 to 17 years whose disease is not adequately controlled with topical prescription therapies or when those therapies are not advisable. IMMUNEXIS can be used with or without topical corticosteroids.

CONTRAINDICATIONS: Known hypersensitivity to IMMUNEXIS or any of its excipients.

WARNINGS AND PRECAUTIONS: Hypersensitivity reactions including anaphylaxis and angioedema have been reported. If a clinically significant hypersensitivity reaction occurs, institute appropriate therapy and discontinue IMMUNEXIS. Pre-treatment evaluation for tuberculosis infection is recommended. Monitor for signs and symptoms of infection. Patients should receive all age-appropriate vaccinations before initiating treatment.

ADVERSE REACTIONS: The most common adverse reactions (incidence ≥5%) are injection site reactions and conjunctivitis.

Please see Full Prescribing Information for IMMUNEXIS®. For healthcare professional use only.

CLARITY Phase 3 — Study Design
CLARITY — Study Design
Randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled, multicenter Phase 3 trial. Pediatric patients aged 6–17 with moderate-to-severe atopic dermatitis (IGA ≥3). After Week 16, all patients received IMMUNEXIS in open-label extension through Week 52. Co-primary endpoints: IGA 0/1 and EASI-75 at Week 16.

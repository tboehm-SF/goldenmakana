Efficacy Summary

  • Co-primary endpoints met: EASI-75 response at Week 16 (72.1% vs. 28.9%; p<0.001) and IGA 0/1 at Week 16 (55.8% vs. 12.3%; p<0.001).
  • Pruritus NRS improvement ≥4 points observed in 68% of treated patients by Week 4, with sustained benefit through Week 52.
  • Open-label extension (CLARITY-OLE) confirmed maintained EASI-75 response of 78% at 156 weeks with no new safety signals.

Safety Profile

  • Most common adverse events (≥5%): injection site reactions (8.2%), nasopharyngitis (6.1%), upper respiratory tract infection (5.4%).
  • Serious adverse event rate comparable between groups (3.1% IMMUNEXIS® vs. 3.4% placebo). No cases of anaphylaxis observed.
  • No clinically meaningful changes in laboratory parameters, ECG findings, or vital signs through 52-week treatment period.

Efficacy Summary

  • Primary endpoint met: 34% relative risk reduction in time to first MACE (cardiovascular death, non-fatal MI, or non-fatal stroke); HR 0.66 (95% CI 0.55–0.79; p<0.001).
  • Dual PCSK9/Lp(a) inhibition achieved LDL-C reduction of 62% and Lp(a) reduction of 91% from baseline sustained through 36 months.
  • Consistent benefit across pre-specified subgroups including age, sex, baseline Lp(a) tertile, diabetes status, and statin use.

Safety Profile

  • Most common adverse events (≥5%): injection site reactions (12.1%), myalgia (6.8%), fatigue (5.2%).
  • No significant increase in new-onset diabetes (HR 1.03; 95% CI 0.87–1.22) or hepatic transaminase elevations (>3× ULN: 1.4% vs. 1.2%).
  • All-cause mortality numerically lower in CARDIOGUARD® arm (4.7% vs. 5.9%), though not powered for this endpoint.

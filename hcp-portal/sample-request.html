Important Notice

The Makana Health Sample Program is available exclusively to licensed healthcare professionals with a valid DEA number and active state medical license.

All sample requests are processed in accordance with 21 CFR Part 203 (Prescription Drug Marketing Act) and applicable state regulations governing the distribution of prescription drug samples.

Samples are intended for complimentary distribution to patients in the normal course of medical practice. Resale or diversion of samples is prohibited under federal law.

By submitting a request, you certify that:

  • You hold a valid, unrestricted license to prescribe in your state
  • Samples will be dispensed only to your patients at no charge
  • You will maintain records of sample receipt and distribution
  • Storage conditions will be maintained per product labeling

Maximum 3 sample packs per product per request. Limit of 12 requests per calendar year per HCP. Program subject to change without notice.

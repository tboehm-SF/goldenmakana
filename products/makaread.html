Key Features

Engineered for Sensitivity & Speed

The MakaRead Pro combines advanced optics, ultra-low background detection, and integrated environmental control to deliver best-in-class performance across all five detection modes.

Quad-Monochromator Optics

Four independent monochromators (2 excitation, 2 emission) enable any wavelength combination without filters. Bandwidth adjustable from 1–20 nm for optimal signal-to-noise in every assay.

Ultra-Low Background Detection

Proprietary optical path design with stray light rejection <0.001% delivers industry-leading luminescence sensitivity down to single-digit attomole levels.

Integrated Environmental Control

Built-in temperature regulation (ambient to 65°C), CO₂/O₂ gas control, and orbital shaking enable kinetic cell-based assays without external incubators.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the advantage of monochromators versus filters for detection?

Monochromators offer continuous wavelength selection across the full 200–1000 nm range, eliminating the need to purchase and maintain filter sets. The MakaRead Pro's quad-monochromator system allows any excitation/emission combination with adjustable bandwidth (1–20 nm), providing greater flexibility for novel fluorophores and multiplexed assays. Filters can offer marginally higher throughput for fixed-wavelength assays, but the monochromator's versatility makes it the superior choice for multi-application labs.

Can the MakaRead Pro perform cell-based kinetic assays?

Yes. The integrated environmental control module provides temperature regulation from ambient to 65°C, CO₂ and O₂ gas control, and orbital shaking. This enables long-term kinetic cell-based assays—including calcium flux, reporter gene, and cytotoxicity monitoring—without the need for external incubators. The fast read speed (<6 seconds per 96-well plate) supports sub-minute kinetic intervals for capturing rapid cellular events.

What plate stackers are compatible with the MakaRead Pro?

The MakaRead Pro is compatible with the Makana 50-plate stacker accessory, which supports all standard SBS-format microplates from 6 to 1536 wells. The stacker includes barcode reading for plate identification and can be configured for walkaway batch processing. For integration with third-party robotic systems and liquid handlers such as the MakaFlow 8000 or MakaStation LX, the MakaRead Pro offers USB and Ethernet connectivity with open API support.

Important Information

INTENDED USE: The MakaPrep® Ultra is designed for the automated isolation and purification of nucleic acids (DNA and RNA) and proteins from a variety of biological sample types. The system is intended for use by trained laboratory personnel in professional research, clinical, and forensic laboratory settings.

REGULATORY STATUS:

  • Research Use Only (RUO): The MakaPrep Ultra Base and Genomics configurations are intended for Research Use Only. Not for use in diagnostic procedures.
  • IVD-Ready: The MakaPrep Ultra Complete configuration is designed to meet IVD requirements when used with validated reagent kits in applicable jurisdictions. Contact your local representative for regulatory status in your region.
  • 21 CFR Part 11: MakaPrep Control software supports 21 CFR Part 11 compliance with electronic signatures, audit trails, and access controls when properly configured.

SAFETY: Always follow established laboratory safety procedures. Wear appropriate personal protective equipment when handling biological samples. Refer to the operator manual for complete safety information, including electrical safety, UV exposure warnings, and chemical handling guidelines.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What sample input types are compatible with MakaPrep Ultra?

The MakaPrep Ultra supports a wide range of sample types including whole blood, buffy coat, tissue (fresh and frozen), FFPE sections, plasma, serum, saliva, buccal swabs, and cell culture lysates. Custom protocols can be developed for additional sample types using the built-in protocol editor. Contact our application scientists for guidance on specialized sample types.

How does automated extraction throughput compare to manual methods?

The MakaPrep Ultra processes 96 genomic DNA samples in 30 minutes, compared to 4-6 hours for manual column-based extraction of the same number. Beyond raw throughput, automation eliminates hands-on time, reduces repetitive strain, and delivers CV values of <5% compared to 15-25% with manual methods. Walk-away time allows operators to focus on downstream analysis and other tasks.

Does MakaPrep Ultra integrate with laboratory information management systems (LIMS)?

Yes. MakaPrep Control v3.0 software provides LIMS integration via standard Ethernet connectivity and supports common data exchange formats including HL7, CSV, and XML. The system includes barcode scanning for sample tracking and can export complete run reports with sample IDs, protocol parameters, and processing timestamps directly to your LIMS. API documentation is available for custom integration development.

Key Features

Advanced Liquid Handling Technology

The MakaFlow 8000 integrates three core technologies to deliver unmatched precision, reliability, and flexibility for automated liquid handling across diverse applications.

Air Displacement Technology

Patented air displacement pipetting ensures consistent and accurate aspiration/dispensation across viscosities, from aqueous buffers to viscous reagents up to 70% glycerol.

Smart Detection System

Real-time liquid level detection, clot detection, and volume verification using capacitive and pressure-based sensors, eliminating failed transfers and ensuring data integrity.

Modular Deck Configuration

Configurable deck with up to 30 positions supporting plates, reservoirs, tip racks, shakers, and thermal modules. Hot-swappable for rapid protocol switching.

Important Information

INTENDED USE: The MakaFlow 8000 Automated Liquid Handling System is intended for use in research, clinical, and pharmaceutical laboratory environments for the automated transfer, dilution, and dispensing of liquid samples and reagents. The system is designed for use by trained laboratory personnel only.

REGULATORY COMPLIANCE: The MakaFlow 8000 is manufactured in compliance with ISO 13485 quality management standards. The system and its associated MakaControl software are designed to support compliance with FDA 21 CFR Part 11 (Electronic Records; Electronic Signatures) and EU GMP Annex 11 (Computerised Systems) when properly configured and validated in the user's environment.

RESEARCH USE ONLY (RUO): Certain configurations and applications of the MakaFlow 8000 may be designated as Research Use Only. RUO products are not intended for use in diagnostic procedures. Users are responsible for validating the system for their specific intended use and for ensuring compliance with applicable local, regional, and national regulations.

SAFETY PRECAUTIONS:

  • Operate the instrument only on a stable, level surface capable of supporting the system weight (52 kg).
  • Do not operate the instrument in the presence of flammable gases or vapors.
  • Keep hands and loose clothing away from moving components during operation.
  • Use only Makana Health-approved tips and consumables to ensure performance specifications.

WARRANTY: The MakaFlow 8000 is covered by a standard 12-month warranty from the date of installation. Extended warranty and service contract options are available. Warranty does not cover damage resulting from misuse, unauthorized modification, or use of non-approved consumables.

For complete technical documentation, safety data sheets, and regulatory information, visit MakanaHealth.com/makaflow/docs or contact your Makana Health representative.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can the MakaFlow 8000 handle 1536-well plates?

The MakaFlow 8000 natively supports 96- and 384-well plate formats. While the deck can physically accommodate 1536-well plates, dispensing into 1536-well format requires the optional 96-head module available with the Ultra configuration. For dedicated 1536-well workflows, contact our application specialists to discuss the optimal configuration for your needs.

What tip types are supported?

The MakaFlow 8000 supports both disposable and washable tip formats. Disposable tips are available in 10 µL, 50 µL, 200 µL, and 1000 µL sizes with optional filter and low-retention variants. Washable steel tips offer a cost-effective alternative for non-critical applications. All tip types are validated for the specified precision and accuracy ranges. Only Makana Health-certified tips are recommended to ensure performance specifications are met.

Is the MakaFlow 8000 compatible with my LIMS?

The MakaFlow 8000 offers broad LIMS compatibility through its SiLA 2 interface and REST API. MakaControl software includes pre-built connectors for major LIMS platforms including LabWare, STARLIMS, Benchling, and Sapio Sciences. For custom or proprietary LIMS systems, our integration team can develop tailored connectors. The Enterprise edition of MakaControl includes advanced LIMS integration features and dedicated integration support.
